About

A short honest bio.

Four years in regulated B2B SaaS. Based in Bangalore, open to relocate to Hyderabad or work remote.

I'm a Product Manager with four years shipping AI-native B2B SaaS across healthcare GRC, clinical workflows, and enterprise retail. My background is in applied product thinking — the unglamorous parts: discovery, prioritization, regulatory sign-off, and killing the things that shouldn't ship.

I've spent most of my career in domains where compliance is existential and the metrics have to be defended, not invented. That's shaped how I work. I prefer controlled automation over autonomous agents, behavior over opinion, and a written kill memo over a polite silence.

Outside of work I read too much philosophy of science, cook irregularly, and keep a small notebook of product failures — mine and other people's — as a running reminder that shipping is the easy part.
